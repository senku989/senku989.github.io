Arduino & IoT: From Idea to Product

What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to devices connected to the internet that collect and exchange data.

What is Arduino?

Arduino is a microcontroller platform used in countless DIY and professional tech projects.

Common Arduino Components

  • Microcontroller (e.g., ATmega328P)
  • Digital and Analog I/O Pins
  • Sensors and Actuators
  • Voltage Booster (for high-voltage outputs)

Going Pro: From Arduino to Product

  1. Prototype with Arduino
  2. Design a circuit with software like EasyEDA
  3. Manufacture a custom PCB
  4. Assemble and test
  5. Package your product for real-world use
